What are they like?

How to make it?

1. Preheat the oven to 350. Spread on a baking sheet or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

2. Place the crumbled pistachios on top and bake for 20-25 minutes (depending on how high the oil heats up or depending on how hot the oil heats up).

3. Remove from oven.
